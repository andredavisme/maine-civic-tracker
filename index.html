Why This Exists

Accountability isn't complaining

When people pay into a system, they're entitled to know what it produces. Tracking outcomes isn't political — it's basic civil responsibility.

Data beats talking points

You can't dismiss documented evidence. Photos, public records, and verified metrics are harder to ignore than a Facebook post.

Roles need names

Money doesn't mismanage itself. Every allocation has someone responsible. This platform makes sure that's visible.

Fix what's broken

If a project isn't producing results, it shouldn't keep getting funded the same way. Transparency is the first step to that change.
