The Conversation Isn't Enough

In 2025, a Maine resident named Nathaniel Fields posted a question that thousands of people feel but can't prove. Maine collects billions from its residents every year. Where does it go? Why aren't we seeing the results?

"The information is out there if you dig for it. The issue is not access. It is clarity and accountability. Most people cannot easily connect spending to real outcomes. That is the gap I am focused on fixing."
— Nathaniel Fields, Maine resident

That gap is exactly what this tracker is built to close. Facebook threads scroll away in 48 hours. The pothole stays. Stop posting. Start documenting.
